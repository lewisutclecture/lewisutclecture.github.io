The 2021 Lecture

We are pleased to announce that our lecturer for Spring 2021, will be Dr. Alister E. McGrath, Andreas Idreos Professor of Science and Religion at the University of Oxford. The lecture will be held online as a live webinar. Check back later for links and updates.

Dr. McGrath is a prominent Christian apologist and a prolific author. For the 50th anniversary of C. S. Lewis's death in 2013, McGrath published several books: C.S. Lewis: A Life. Eccentric Genius, Reluctant Prophet (Tyndale House), The Intellectual World of C. S. Lewis. (Oxford: Wiley-Blackwell, 2013), and If I Had Lunch with C. S. Lewis: Exploring the Ideas of C. S. Lewis on the Meaning of Life (Tyndale). The topic of his lecture will be: "C. S. Lewis: How I Discovered Him - and What I Learned from Him." The webinar will be held at 5:00 p.m. (EDST) on April 7. The webinar lecture is co-sponsored by the Committee for the Annual C. S. Lewis Lecture at UTC and the Christian Study Center of Chattanooga.
